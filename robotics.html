About the subject

Our interactive dual-education platform helps students grow in the field of robotics by connecting them directly with real companies, engineers, and modern training environments. Through the platform, learners gain access to hands-on experience with industrial robots, robotic arms, automation systems, sensors, and programming tools used in today’s industry.




Students can explore learning modules, practice robot programming in virtual simulations, and work on real robotics projects provided by partner companies. Employers share real tasks—such as building robotic mechanisms, programming automated systems, or integrating machine-vision solutions—allowing students to develop practical skills that match industry needs.




By bringing together schools, students, and robotics companies, the platform makes it easier to learn, train, and build a future career in robotics and automation. It helps young talents gain confidence, receive guidance from professionals, and prepare for high-tech careers in a rapidly growing field.




Through our platform, students can easily connect with robotics mentors, instructors, and employers using built-in communication tools. Each company and mentor has a profile with contact options such as email, direct messaging, or video calls, making it simple to ask questions, receive feedback, or apply for internships.
